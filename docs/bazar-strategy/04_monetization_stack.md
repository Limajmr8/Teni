## LAYER 1: HERITAGE MARKETPLACE

| Revenue Stream | Mechanism | Target Margin/Fee | Hormozi Framework |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Inventory margin** | Buy at artisan price, sell at 2–3x markup | **50–65% gross margin** | // HORMOZI: Value Equation — reduce buyer's effort (curation) and time delay (inventory-ready). Premium justified by scarcity + story. |
| **International shipping** | Markup on DHL/FedEx rates | 15–25% markup on actual shipping | // HORMOZI: Bundled service. Buyer pays one price, BAZAR handles export docs, customs, packaging. |
| **Artisan Spotlight** | Paid editorial feature on BAZAR Heritage page | ₹2,000–₹5,000/month per artisan | // HORMOZI: Lead generation for artisans. Guaranteed eyeballs on their profile. Pay for distribution, not creation. |
| **BAZAR Quality Mark** | Certification fee for non-BAZAR sellers to use "BAZAR Verified" stamp | ₹5,000/year per seller | // HORMOZI: Dream Outcome — seller gets trust badge. Reduce buyer's perceived risk. Licensing play. |
| **Heritage Gift Boxes** | Curated gift bundles (Diwali, Christmas, Corporate) | **70% gross margin** | // HORMOZI: Grand Slam Offer — bundle king chili + shawl + smoked pork + story booklet. Single purchase replaces 4 separate buying decisions. |

**Layer 1 Unit Economics (per Heritage Box):**
```
Artisan cost (contents):     ₹800
Packaging + seal + QR:       ₹150
Photography/editorial:       ₹50 (amortized)
FSSAI/compliance overhead:   ₹30
TOTAL COGS:                  ₹1,030

Selling price:               ₹2,999
Shipping (buyer pays):       ₹200–₹500 additional
Gross margin:                ₹1,969 (65.7%)
```

## LAYER 2: SUMO SYNDICATE LOGISTICS

| Revenue Stream | Mechanism | Fee/Margin | Hormozi Framework |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Seat-Rate logistics fee** | Customer pays delivery fee, split between Sumo driver, counter, BAZAR | ₹50–₹150 per parcel. BAZAR keeps ₹10–₹25 | // HORMOZI: Reduce effort — customer doesn't negotiate with driver. Guaranteed delivery vs hope. |
| **Insurance margin** | Embedded "Parcel Protection" in delivery fee | ₹50 charged, ₹20 actual premium = **₹30 margin** | // HORMOZI: Reduce perceived risk. "Your ₹5,000 shawl is insured against damage." Risk reversal. |
| **Digital LR fee** | ₹10–₹20 per LR generated | BAZAR keeps ₹3–₹6 per LR | // HORMOZI: Transaction fee on infrastructure BAZAR built once. Zero marginal cost after setup. |
| **Syndicate Counter SaaS** | Monthly subscription for counter management software | ₹299–₹499/month per counter | // HORMOZI: Continuity revenue. Counter gets: manifest tracking, daily settlement reports, customer CRM. Pain of switching = they lose all data. |

**Seat-Rate Pricing Model:**
```
Route: Mokokchung → Kohima (typical)
Traditional: ₹50 "chai-pani" with zero accountability

BAZAR Seat-Rate:
  Small parcel (< 2kg):    ₹80
  Medium (2–5kg):          ₹120  
  Large (5–10kg):          ₹180
  
Revenue Split:
  Driver:          50% (₹40/₹60/₹90)
  Counter:         20% (₹16/₹24/₹36)
  BAZAR:           15% (₹12/₹18/₹27)
  Insurance pool:  15% (₹12/₹18/₹27)
  
Driver incentive vs status quo:
  Old: ₹50 flat, no guarantee of parcel volume
  New: ₹40–₹90 per parcel, but guaranteed volume 
       via BAZAR aggregation. 10 parcels/trip = ₹400–₹900.
  NET GAIN for driver: 2–3x current income from parcels.
```

## LAYER 3: HYPERLOCAL

| Revenue Stream | Mechanism | Fee/Margin | Hormozi Framework |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Dark store margin** | Buy wholesale, sell retail | **20–30% gross margin** on groceries/essentials | // HORMOZI: Speed + convenience premium. Same product, but delivered in 30 min vs 1 hour walk to market. |
| **Delivery fee** | Per-order delivery charge | ₹15–₹30 per delivery | // HORMOZI: Direct monetization of effort. Reduced to ₹0 for BAZAR Pass subscribers. |
| **BAZAR Pass** | Monthly subscription | **₹299/month**: free delivery + 5% discount on all orders | // HORMOZI: Continuity model. LTV of subscriber = 6–8x non-subscriber. Locks in repeat behavior. |
| **Scheduled Batch Premium** | Charge ₹5 MORE for scheduled (reliable) vs standard | ₹5 premium per scheduled delivery | // HORMOZI: Counterintuitive pricing. Certainty > speed for many buyers. "Guaranteed 3pm delivery" is worth more than "maybe 30 min." |
| **Seller commission** | Commission on BAZAR MARKET (marketplace) orders | 8–12% of order value | // HORMOZI: Marketplace take rate. Seller gets demand, BAZAR gets margin. |

**BAZAR Pass Economics:**
```
Monthly subscription:           ₹299
Avg orders/month (subscriber):  12
Delivery fee saved:             12 × ₹25 = ₹300
5% discount on avg ₹500 order:  12 × ₹25 = ₹300
Total perceived savings:        ₹600 (2x the subscription price)

BAZAR's actual cost:
  Delivery cost saved:  12 × ₹20 = ₹240 (batch delivery cost)
  Discount funded:      ₹300
  Total cost:           ₹540
  
NET per subscriber:     ₹299 - ₹540 = -₹241/month

BUT: Subscriber orders 12x/month vs non-subscriber 3x/month.
Dark store margin per order: ₹100 (on ₹500 AOV at 20% margin)
Subscriber margin: 12 × ₹100 = ₹1,200
Non-subscriber margin: 3 × ₹100 = ₹300

NET SUBSCRIBER VALUE: ₹1,200 + ₹299 - ₹540 = ₹959/month
NET NON-SUB VALUE: ₹300 + (3 × ₹25 delivery fee) = ₹375/month

// HORMOZI: The subscription LOSES money in isolation but 
// 2.5x the customer lifetime value through frequency lock-in.
```

## LAYER 4: IMT / EXPORT (2028+ Revenue)

| Revenue Stream | Mechanism | Fee/Margin | Hormozi Framework |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Exporter of Record margin** | BAZAR handles export docs, customs, shipping | 10–15% of FOB value | // HORMOZI: Reduce effort to zero for artisan. They make the product, BAZAR does everything else. |
| **Export documentation fee** | APEDA registration, NPOP cert, phytosanitary cert | ₹5,000–₹15,000 per artisan onboarding | // HORMOZI: One-time setup fee. Artisan couldn't do this alone. Remove the biggest obstacle. |
| **GI verification service (B2B)** | Foreign buyer pays to verify authenticity of NE India products | $50–$200 per verification | // HORMOZI: Reduce perceived risk for foreign buyer. "Is this real Naga King Chili?" Yes, here's the chain of custody. |
| **Consignment facilitation** | Connect NE artisans with Bangkok/Singapore retailers | 5–8% ongoing commission | // HORMOZI: Recurring revenue on relationships BAZAR built. |
