# TENI Platform - Future Roadmap

This document lists deferred features and enhancements for the TENI platform.

## 1. AI & Intelligence
- **AI Recommendations:** Personalized product suggestions based on buyer behavior and seasonal trends in Mokokchung.
- **Dynamic Pricing:** Automatic price adjustments for dark store items based on demand and expiry dates.
- **Automated Runner Dispatch:** AI-driven assignment of runners based on proximity, historical speed, and battery levels.

## 2. Communication & Community
- **In-App Chat:** Real-time chat between buyers and sellers, and buyers and runners.
- **Multi-language Support:** Localize the UI into Ao (Mokokchung's primary language) and Nagamese.
- **Community Feed:** A dedicated section for town announcements, festival specials, and "Village Highlights".

## 3. Operations & Logistics
- **Advanced Seller Analytics:** Deep insights for marketplace sellers regarding their peak selling times, customer retention, and competitor benchmarking.
- **Warehouse Automation:** Integration with barcode scanners and automated inventory restocking alerts.
- **Multi-Town Expansion:** Dedicated dark stores and ambassador panels for Kohima, Dimapur, and Wokha.

## 4. Growth & Monetization
- **Loyalty Program:** Tiered benefits for high-frequency buyers and "Top Rated" sellers.
- **B2B Supply:** Sourcing marketplace products (like smoked meats) in bulk for regional restaurants.
- **Ad Platform:** Self-serve ad placement for local businesses beyond just product boosting.
